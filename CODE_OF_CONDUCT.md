# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community. Treat everyone
with respect, assume good intent, give constructive feedback, and avoid
personal attacks, discrimination, or intimidation.

Report concerns privately to the maintainers through the contact method in
[SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md). Maintainers will acknowledge reports promptly,
protect confidentiality where possible, investigate fairly, and may warn,
remove content, or restrict participation when needed.
