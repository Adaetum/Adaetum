# Governance

Adaetum is maintainer-led and contribution-driven. Maintainers set release
scope, approve architecture changes, and protect security boundaries. Material
changes to bootstrap phases, secret authority, profile contracts, or
supported providers require two maintainer approvals and an architecture note.

Maintainers follow the [triage and release process](docs/maintainer-process.md)
for issue review, release evidence, and public communication. Inactive
maintainers may be rotated out after a private check-in; active sustained
contributors may be invited to maintainership by consensus.
